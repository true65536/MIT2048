How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles of the same college touch, they merge into one!

Created by K Singh. Heavily inspired by and based almost entirely on GetMIT! by Michelle Gu and Get Caltech by Naveen Arun. The game and code originates from 2048 by Gabriele Cirulli. and 1024 by Veewo Studio which is conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
